Benefits

Benefits we provide to our clients

Boosted productivity and efficiency

Optimizing the kinematics and energy usage of robots can significantly boost efficiency and maximize productivity.

Enhanced system flexibility

With some system and tool adjustments, a single robot can be repurposed for multiple applications, maximizing its value and versatility.

Increased product quality

Robotic systems exhibit higher accuracy and repeatability in their work compared to humans leading to enhanced product quality after process establishment and optimization.

Increased cost savings

As labor costs continue to rise, the affordability of industrial robots is on the rise, with prices decreasing over time. Studies predict that by 2025, the use of industrial robots will result in a 22% reduction in labor costs.

Advanced data tracking and analytics

Data tracking and analysis of production output, energy, speed and material usage can be facilitated through PLC, SCADA, MES and ERP systems to enable efficient planning.

Reduced waste

Optimizing robots to minimize material usage leads to a decrease in waste within production facilities.

Freeing workers for higher-value tasks

Industrial robots excel in repetitive and monotonous tasks, freeing human labor for more meaningful and creative endeavors.

Services we provide for our clients

Palletization

We are designing custom palletization stations that can handle a wide range of product weights and sizes with the possibility of incorporating robots and additional axes, such as linear tracks.

Bending

Industrial robots can increase productivity by serving as loaders/unloaders for bending machines, placing and removing metal sheets during the metal forming process.

Custom grippers

Custom made tools and grippers are often necessary for industrial robots, as they are tailored to the specific application and can optimize automation processes.

Custom cells

A custom-made robotic cell is often the best solution to fit the specific demands and constraints of an industrial facility.

Packing

Industrial robots can efficiently and quickly replace humans in monotonous packing tasks, with the use of different types of robots and grippers, a wide range of products can be packed.

Milling

6-axis robots and external positioners provide flexibility in milling applications. Though less accurate than CNC machines, they offer a larger working range and are suitable for materials like wood, spongy and gypsum materials. Robotic milling can also create complex geometries using CAM software.
